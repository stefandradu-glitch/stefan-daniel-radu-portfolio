About Me

I'm a BEng Aerospace Engineering graduate from the University of Hertfordshire (2025), with a strong focus on structural design, CAE simulation, and UAV systems development.

My degree project centred on composite UAV wing structural optimisation, combining FEA simulation with real-world design constraints. Across my studies I led teams of up to 8 members on multi-disciplinary aircraft and rover design challenges.

Outside of academia, I have a deep passion for Formula 1 — the sport's relentless pursuit of aerodynamic and structural performance mirrors exactly the kind of precision engineering I want to build a career in. I'm equally drawn to the defence sector, where the demands for quality, reliability, and innovation are uncompromising.

Above all, I believe that quality of work is non-negotiable. Whether it's a simulation, a CAD model, or an analysis report, I hold every piece of work to the highest standard — because in engineering, the details matter.
