About Me

I'm a BEng Aerospace Engineering graduate from the University of Hertfordshire (2025), with a strong focus on structural design, CAE simulation, and UAV systems development.

My degree project centred on composite UAV wing structural optimisation, combining FEA simulation with real-world design constraints. Across my studies I led teams of up to 8 members on multi-disciplinary aircraft and rover design challenges.

I'm passionate about aerospace and defence, automotive engineering, and pushing the boundaries of what CAD and simulation tools can achieve — whether that's optimising aerostructures or designing from scratch in Fusion 360.
